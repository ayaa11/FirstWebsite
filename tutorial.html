Milk Shake

How to make a delicious milk shake ?!

Milkshakes one of the most refreshing drinks, especially in the summer time. Also, it is nutritious because it has milk which is the main ingredient. It can be healthy as well, when you add some fruit which is a really good because of the vitamins that might has. Moreover, it could contain sugar that provides human body with energy. One of the things that i am good at is making desserts. It is nice to have dessert after a long, exhausted day. It makes you feel relaxing and take all the stress away. Specially, when people are having conversation and eating some sweets. Therefore, I would like to tell you about how to make an easy milkshake. This is the simplest steps you can follow in order to make it. Have some relaxation from stressful and enjoy your milkshake. After that, I had uploaded a video about five outrageously delicious milkshakes.

  1. 1- Start to put two scoop of Vanilla icecream and one glass of milk that would be enough for one glass of milk shake.

  2. 2- Add three small spoons of sugar.

  3. 3- Cubes of ice, depends on what you like. I usually put five cubes.

  4. 4- The flavour which is Lotus (choose the flavour that you like). Most of the times i choose to have whether Lotus or Oreo, I like both of them.

  5. 5- Now is the step of blending everything together for about three minutes.

  6. 6- The last step which is enjoying the amazing shake.
